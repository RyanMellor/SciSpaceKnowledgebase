# Alcohol

## Descriptive

* **Formula:** -OH
* **Nomenclature:** hydroxy-, -ol
* **H-bond:** Acceptor and Donor
* **Polar:** Yes
* **Geometry:** Bent,$$109\degree$$

## Physicochemical

* **pKa:** 17
